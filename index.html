About the event

The IT FEST of WMO IG Arts and Science College Kappumchal Panamaram is an exciting event that promises to showcase the talents of the students in the field of Information Technology. The IT FEST of WMO IG Arts and Science College Kappumchal Panamaram is an event that promises to be a memorable experience for all attendees. We invite all students who are passionate about technology and have a desire to excel in their field to join us and take part in this exciting event.

About Us

WMOIG COLLEGE, Cherukattoor, Wayanad is committed to enrich the young generation by empowering them. We provide holistic education that enables them to actively participate in community life. As we know, education is a crucial factor that contributes to nation-building, so for the past ninety years we have been educating young people from different strata of our society. We established our institution by keeping in mind the exact concept of learning to meet the needs of the present generation and we equip them with the necessary skill sets to meet the different challenges in life.
As the mission of WMO organization, WMOIGAC has been laid the foundation by fostering a culture of responsibility, so that students engage with the world around them to take on responsibilities while at college and pursue roles that seek to make a positive contribution after graduation.

DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS

The Department of COMPUTER APPLICATION at WMO IG Arts and Science College Kappumchal Wayanad is dedicated to providing its students with a comprehensive education in computer applications and information technology. With a focus on hands-on learning, the department strives to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

As part of this mission, the department is proud to announce the upcoming IT Fest, Tiqnia2023. This exciting event will be held on 23rd February 2023, and is organized entirely by the students of the BCA department. With 8 events and cash prizes, Tiqnia2023 promises to be an exciting showcase of the talent and creativity of the students of the BCA department.

Whether you're a student of the Computer Science department or simply interested in technology, Tiqnia2023 is an event you won't want to miss. With opportunities to participate, learn and compete, this IT Fest is a perfect opportunity for anyone with an interest in technology to showcase their skills and connect with others who share their passion. So join us on 23rd February 2023 for Tiqnia2023, an exciting and engaging IT Fest that promises to be a celebration of technology, creativity and the passion of the students of the BCA department at WMO IG Arts and Science College Kappumchal Wayanad.
